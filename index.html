Who I am

Defense-minded. Build-driven.

I lead Cybersecurity & Network Infrastructure at the Federal Mortgage Bank of Nigeria — a CBN-regulated institution — where I own the firewall estate, endpoint protection, SIEM, and network operations across 40+ branches and roughly 1,300 staff. I've held that mandate since September 2018, through real incidents, real audits, and real scale.

Outside of that mandate, I design and ship the products I wish existed, through my own company, ARX AMANI Technologies — including an enterprise secrets vault and IT asset management platform built for Nigerian financial institutions, plus a suite of AI-assisted vulnerability assessment and penetration testing tools.

My background is unconventional — I came to security through physics, not a bootcamp — and that shows up as a habit of reasoning from first principles rather than following playbooks. I blend offensive thinking (how systems actually get broken) with practical engineering (how to ship something that closes the gap), because the best defenses are the ones that get deployed, not just designed.
